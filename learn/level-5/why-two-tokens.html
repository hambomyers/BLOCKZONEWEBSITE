Why Two Tokens?

In blockchain gaming, a single token often leads to problems. Let's explore why Blockzone uses two tokens: QUARTERS and DUST.

The Single Token Trap

Many blockchain games use a single token for both gameplay and investment. This creates volatility, as players and investors have conflicting interests.

Example: Axie Infinity's SLP token crashed by 99.5% because it was used for both rewards and breeding, leading to unsustainable demand.

Our Solution: Two Tokens

Blockzone separates gameplay and investment with two tokens:

QUARTERS (QTR)

The Gaming Currency

  • Always worth $0.25
  • Used for gameplay and rewards
  • Stable and predictable

DUST

The Investment Token

  • Earned by playing
  • Used for governance and perks
  • Can appreciate in value

Real-World Example

Imagine Sarah plays Neon Drop:

  1. She wins $0.15 in QUARTERS.
  2. She earns 150 DUST as a bonus.
  3. She uses DUST to unlock new features.

This system ensures that gameplay remains fun and rewards are stable, while DUST provides long-term value for investors.

The "Aha!" Moment

By separating gameplay and investment, Blockzone creates a sustainable ecosystem where everyone wins.
